Frequently Asked Questions




What is ColourWay ?


ColourWay is an app/website that allows people to share the things that make them

different, as well as the things that make them the same, while traveling.

Users do this by sharing photos, safety tips, and reviews of all aspects of travel, with

their phones, tablets, and laptops/PC's.



How much does the app cost?


The app is free in both the Google Play store and the Apple App Store.


How did the idea for ColourWay come about?


In doing some research, we found that about seventeen percent of Black people in the

US take international trips every year, and we spend $48 billion alone, which is a

significant number. That's almost half of our $1 trillion buying power. However, when

I watch travel commercials and view travel advertisements, and watched travel-themed

shows on televison, I see a less than desirable amount of black faces. It is my home

that this app brings people together and gives them a voice to not only show thw world

that travel is something we're not only interested in being a part of, but that we are

actually a part of.




What other services are you compatible with?


We hope to become compatible with photo sharing sources (Flickr, Instagram, VSCO, etc.)

with social media sites (Facebook, Google+, Twitter, etc.), and with location sharing sites

(Swarm).
