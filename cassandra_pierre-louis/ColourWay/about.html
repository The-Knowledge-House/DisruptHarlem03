About Us






What is ColourWay?



Simply put, ColourWay is a way to share your travel experiences with people just like you, or

nothing like you. ColourWay lets you tell us who you are, and who you identify as, and tailors

your suggestions, and ultimately your travel experience, with those things in mind.


ColourWay gives you the tools necessary to have a safer more inclusive travel experience through

travel deals and suggestions, based on the budget you provide us, the ability to form travel groups

based on similarities or interests, in-app navigation loaded with safety features and alerts,especially

for women, and peer reviews from people just like you!





Cassandra L. Pierre-Louis

(CEO & Founder)




Cassandra Pierre-Louis is the CEO & Founder of ColourWay, a

community of people who come together to share travel experiences

and share tip and reviews, amongst other things, on a platform

that promotes inclusion in relation to travel


An avid traveler, Cassandra has always approached her travel planning with a bit of trepidation.

She has always had concerns about traveling as a woman, but more specifically as a black woman.
