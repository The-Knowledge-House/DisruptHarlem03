WHO



For everyone.



Simply put, ColorWay is a way to share your travel experiences with people just like you, or nothing like you.

ColorWay lets you tell us who you are, and who you identify as, and tailors your suggestions taking those things into account.





WHAT



ColorWay gives you the tools necessary to have a safer more inclusive travel experience, through

travel deals relative to a provided budget, navigation loaded with safety features, especially for

women, and peer reviews from people just like you!
