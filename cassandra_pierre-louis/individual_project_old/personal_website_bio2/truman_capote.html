Truman Capote

Truman Capote was one of the most reknowned writers of the 1960s.

He was most known for his major piece of work,'Breakfast at Tiffany's', which was later made into a popular film starring Audrey Hepburn.

He was also known for'In Cold Blood', a book about the four-part murder of the Cuttler family in a small town of Kansas, which he wrote with his good friend Harper Lee

To me, the greatest pleasure of writing is not what it's about, but the inner music that words make.
