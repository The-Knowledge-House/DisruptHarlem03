Disrupt Harlem


Disrupt Harlem is a Harlem based program aimed at teaching young adults between the ages of 18-25 how to code in three different languages. Disrupt Harlem also teaches students entrepreneurial skills, as well as professional developing skills including:

  • Resume/Cover Letter building
  • Pitching
  • Networking


Upon completing this program, students of the program will be equipped with front-end development skills that will allow them to become more marketable and allow them to enter into their prospective careers.
