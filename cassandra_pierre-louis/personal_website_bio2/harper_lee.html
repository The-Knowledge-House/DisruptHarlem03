Harper Lee

Harper Lee was an reknowned writer known for her major best-seller, 'To Kill a Mockingbird'.

Her story, which dealt with the issues of racial inequality, later won a Pulitzer Prize.

She was a close friend of Truman Capote, another major author of her time, with whom she shared a unique bond.

She later copllaborated with to document the story of four-part killing of the Cuttler family in a small town in Kansas.
