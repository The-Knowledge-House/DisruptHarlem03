Capote & Lee: In Cold Blood

This website contains two site pages of two of the most prolific authors of the 1960s: Truman Capote and Harper Lee.

Both of them were amazing authors in their own rigts, and worked in different genres

They were linked through their friendship.

After having achieving much success in their respective careers, Lee later helped Capote write a story together about the killings of four members of a Kansas family in the 1960s.
